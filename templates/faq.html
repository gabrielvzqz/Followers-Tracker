Which format should I use to export my Instagram data?
You should select the JSON format when exporting your Instagram data. This format is compatible with our tool and allows it to read your followers and followings correctly.
How to Export Your Instagram Data
  1. Open Instagram and log in.
  2. Go to Settings → Accounts Centre → Your Information and Permissions.
  3. Select Export your information.
  4. Select JSON format and request your data.
  5. Wait for Instagram to prepare your file (you’ll get an email).
  6. Download the ZIP file and upload it here.
